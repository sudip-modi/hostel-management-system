A good hostel should provide a safe, comfortable, and supportive environment for its residents.

The main facilities of our hostel includes:
  • Security: The hostel should have adequate security measures such as CCTV cameras, security guards, and secure locking systems to ensure the safety of the residents.
  • Cleanliness: The hostel should be clean and well-maintained, with regular cleaning and maintenance of common areas and individual rooms.
  • Comfortable sleeping arrangements: The hostel should provide comfortable beds, pillows, and blankets for residents to ensure a good night's sleep.
  • Study area: A study area or quiet room should be available for residents to study and work on their assignments.
  • Recreation facilities: Common recreational areas such as TV rooms, gaming zones, and sports facilities should be available for residents to relax and unwind.
  • Laundry facilities: The hostel should provide laundry facilities, such as washing machines, to enable residents to wash their clothes.
  • Kitchen facilities: The hostel should provide a clean and well-equipped kitchen for residents to cook their own meals.
  • Wi-Fi: A good hostel should provide fast and reliable internet connectivity to allow residents to stay connected with their family and friends and complete their online work.
  • 24-hour water and electricity supply: The hostel should provide uninterrupted water and electricity supply to ensure the comfort and convenience of the residents. Medical facilities: The hostel should have first-aid facilities and easy access to medical facilities in case of an emergency.
